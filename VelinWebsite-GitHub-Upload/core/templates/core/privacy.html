Privacy Policy

Privacy designed for public profiles.

Velin only publishes information that the profile owner chooses to make public. Accounts stay private by default until the owner verifies their email and explicitly publishes the profile.

What We Collect

We store the account email, profile details entered by the user, consent timestamps, and any uploaded profile media such as photos or business pitch files.

Why We Collect It

The data exists so Velin can host the public profile behind the NFC card, manage secure account access, and give the owner control over their own published identity.

User Control

Users can export their stored profile data, unpublish their public profile, or submit a deletion request from the dashboard.

Profile Visibility

Public profile pages only show the details a user chooses to share, and that visibility can be updated at any time from the account dashboard.
